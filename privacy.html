Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

1. Data Collection

QPUStatus is a transparency-focused project. We do not require account creation or personal information to access our telemetry. However, we use third-party tools to ensure site security and analyze performance.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for DNS management, SSL, and DDoS protection. Cloudflare may process your IP address and browser metadata to ensure secure access to our network.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track general traffic trends (e.g., which QPUs are most viewed). This data is anonymized.
  • Microsoft Clarity: We use Microsoft Clarity to capture how you interact with our dashboard (heatmaps and session recordings) to improve the UI/UX. All sensitive data is masked.

2. Cookies

These third-party services use cookies to store information about your preferences and session. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without losing access to our telemetry.

3. Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy, please reach out via our Contact Page.
