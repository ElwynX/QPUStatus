SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

Aria-1 represents IonQ's commercial-grade trapped ion quantum computer. It utilizes a vacuum chamber where Ytterbium ions are trapped in a linear chain using electromagnetic fields. Computations are performed by directing precisely tuned laser beams at individual ions to manipulate their quantum states.
