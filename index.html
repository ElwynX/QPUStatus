Dashboard FAQ

Why is Azure measured in minutes, but AWS in tasks?

Cloud providers expose their APIs differently. AWS Braket exposes the raw integer count of pending quantum tasks currently in the queue. Azure Quantum abstracts this and provides an estimated wait time (in minutes) based on historical job processing speeds.

What does it mean when a machine goes OFFLINE?

Unlike standard web servers, quantum processors require frequent recalibration, laser tuning, and cooling cycles. An "OFFLINE" status on our dashboard typically indicates routine daily maintenance or calibration windows rather than a hardware failure.
